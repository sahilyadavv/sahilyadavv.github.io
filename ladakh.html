Road Trip To Ladakh 2018 (Delhi-Manali-Leh-Manali-Delhi) - 12 Days

  • Departure from Delhi by Volvo


    Over Night Journey from Delhi to Manali by Volvo

  • Manali - Local Sightseeing

    Arrival Manali, Gear Checkup & Manali Sightseeing We board the awaiting Volvo coaches and take an overnight ride to Manali. Upon arrival, a traditional welcome followed by introduction with the Tour leader, followed by allotment of rooms. Followed by a safety & rescue session by a Professional. Evening welcomes you to a campfire, followed by a tour briefing and some flagship celebrations followed by dinner.

  • MANALI TO SARCHU via Jispa

    You wake up early in the morning after breakfast we allot the participants their respective bikes etc. to ride on the world's highest Motorable road - the Manali-Leh highway. As soon as you leave Manali, the climb for the 13,051 feet high Rohtang Pass starts. After Rohtang, the road descends into the Chandra valley, passing through Khoksar and on to as Sarchu.

  • SARCHU TO LEH

    Today you ride from the lush green landscape to the stark moonscape which Ladakh is famous for. A very scenic passing through small lakes, waterfalls and streams. Undoubtedly the best stretch on the ManaliLeh highway, this section of the Manali-Leh highway will make you feel as if you're on a roller coaster ride climbing up and down from one Himalayan pass to another. You ride up and down three high altitude passes, Baralacha-la Lachlung -La (16,616 feet), Nakee-La (15,547 feet). Overnight stay in Leh & Dinner will be served

  • LEH LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

    After breakfast we start our ride toward Shanti Stupa its one of the magnificient area in Ladakh This Day gonna very adventure and you will be witness of Galaxy from your Naked eyes in A dark night. Enroute visit Lamayuru, one of the oldest Monastery of Ladakh and enjoy the view of Moon Landscape. Magnetic Hills, Pathar Sahib Gurudwara and Hall of Fame museum. Again here with view we stay overnight with our fellow group members.

  • LEH to NUBRA via Khardungla

    After breakfast, this day is very important for us after breakfast we will start our ride early morning 5:30 Am toward the 350km long roads that stretches to the alluring Nubra Valley. The most interesting part of the journey is crossing through second highest Motorable road Khardung La Pass. After celeb Khardung-La pass, you descend into the dramatically different landscape of the Nubra. Overnight stay in Best Camps at Nubra & Dinner.

  • NUBRA to PANGONG LAKE

    Today after breakfast, we will be taking one of the most uncommon routes in Ladakh area via Shayok village which was opened recently for the tourist. While riding u will defiantly enjoy the scenery view of mountains and road and this route is also known for waterfalls. Overnight stay in Best Camp at Pangong & Dinner.

  • PANGONG LAKE TO LEH

    leaving this enchanting lake of 3Idiots, we move back to our stupendous destination Leh. With 170km to cover we will be crossing many imaginary villages with local people to watch around. Reaching Leh after the journey of 6-7 hours we will be staying overnight in Leh again & Dinner will be served.

  • LEH to SARCHU

    Today, you depart for Sarchu, reach over there and enjoy the evening. The distance in Ladakh region from Sarchu to Leh is 260 km (160 mi). Stay at Sarchu & Dinner will be served.

  • SARCHU to MANALI

    After Breakfast leave for Manali, the scenery started unfolding around us. Grass-covered mountain slopes looked great with the numerous tiny purple, pink and yellow flowers mixing magically with the green. The road is not particularly wide and the ascent is slow and treacherous. Over Night stay In Manali & Dinner will be served..
