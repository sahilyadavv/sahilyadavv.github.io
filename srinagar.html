Trip To Kashmir – Its Heaven On Earth | Kashmir View 2018 - 7 Days

  • Board the Train - Delhi - Jammu


    Gather at New Delhi Railway Station/Bus Stand to board your train to Jammu. Overnight at baord.

  • Arrive Jammu

    Arrive Jammu Railway Station board your bus and proceed to Srinagar. Reach Srinagar till evening. Check in the Hotel. Later enjoy the SHIKARA RIDE on Dal Lake, where you visit the floating vegetable gardens. While riding the Shikara there are plenty of opportunities for bird watching with plentiful species including Kingfisher, Little Bittern, Common Pariah Kites, Grebe etc. Dinner & overnight Stay in Houseboat.

  • Srinagar - Gulmarg - Srinagar

    After breakfast proceed to Gulmarg (8700 ft i.e. 2652 mts), known as ‘Meadow of flowers. You can enjoy the famous GANDOLA RIDE to Aparwath. Come back to Srinagar till evening. Dinner & Overnight stay at the Hotel in Srinagar.

  • Srinagar - Pahalgham

    After breakfast check out from the hotel and proceed to Pahalgam (2133 mts) also known as ‘The valley of Shepherds or Trekker’s Paradise. On the way visit Awantipura ruins, Saffron fields. Dinner & Overnight stay at hotel in Pahalgham. DJ Night with Bonfire

  • Pahalgham - Local Sightseeing

    Day free for Leisure. Take Horses and explore the new places such as Baisaran, Kashmir Valley. Dinner & Overnight stay at hotel in Pahalgham.

  • Arrive Delhi

    Reach Delhi in morning with unforgettable memories of Kashmir Valley. Tour concludes. TravelBreed thanks you for your patronage
